Employee Engagement

Team Trust & Engagement Intelligence for People Managers.

People Analytics AI Agent LLM HR Tech

The Problem

Managers see scores. They don't know what to do.

Organisations invest heavily in engagement surveys — annual or pulse — but the output typically stops at reports and dashboards. Managers, who are the real owners of team culture, are left without clear guidance on what actions to take. This is mainly because many of them are first-time managers and need guidance.

This creates three key gaps:

1

Insight → Action Gap: Managers see engagement scores but don't know how to translate them into day-to-day leadership actions.

2

Generic Interventions: Existing tools provide broad recommendations (e.g., "improve recognition") that lack context from actual employee feedback.

3

No Measurement of Impact: There is no clear way to track whether interventions actually improve engagement or reduce attrition risk.

Solution Overview

EngageIQ — a weekly operating system for managers.

EngageIQ is a manager-first engagement intelligence layer that converts employee feedback into specific, actionable, and measurable interventions. Instead of being a reporting tool for HR, it acts as a weekly operating system for managers — helping them:

  • Understand what's happening in their team
  • Know exactly what actions to take
  • Improve trust and engagement through continuous feedback loops

Prototype Delivers

What the prototype does today.

  • Tracks weekly team trust & engagement trends across key drivers, highlighting the most critical areas of decline.
  • Generates AI-powered insights that explain what's driving engagement changes within the team.
  • Surfaces employee-level pulse signals, including lowest driver and specific feedback for context.
  • Provides AI-powered targeted coaching guidance for each employee — from quick suggestions to detailed conversation playbooks.
  • Drives execution through a weekly manager action tracker with clear interventions and ready-to-use communication prompts.

Metrics That Will Move

How success gets measured.

Metric Impact
Attrition Risk Score Reduction in Attrition Risk Score by moving employees from high-risk to lower-risk categories over time.
Weekly Pulse Participation Rate Improved guidance from manager leads to increased engagement from team on trust factors.
